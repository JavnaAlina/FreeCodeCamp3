Information about FCIT

Faculty of Computer Information Technologies (FCIT) of Ternopil National Economic University (TNEU) was founded in 1994. The first dean of the faculty was the Doctor of Technical Sciences, professor Anatoliy Sachenko. Currently, the faculty is headed by Doctor of Technical Sciences, Professor Mykola Dyvak.
